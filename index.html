How to use?

Welcome to Wise Wealth! Here is a quick guide on how to use the app

  1. Add Transactions: In the form on the left, fill out the fields:
    • Description: Provide the reason or name of the transaction.
    • Amount: Enter the amount of the transaction. Use positive numbers for income and expenses.
    • Category: Select the category of the transaction.
    • Type: Choose whether the transaction is income or an expense.
    After filling out all the fields, click the "Submit Data" button to add the transaction.

  2. View Transactions: The added transactions will be displayed on the right. You can view the transactions for the current month and navigate between months using the "<" and ">" buttons.

  3. Balance: The total balance will be automatically updated based on the transactions added. The balance is displayed at the top left and will be highlighted in green for positive values and red for negative values.

If you have any questions or need support, feel free to click the Support button.
